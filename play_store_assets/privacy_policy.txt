# Privacy Policy for PDF Hub

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

## Introduction

PDF Hub ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our PDF processing application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Files you choose to process (PDFs, images, documents)
- User preferences and settings
- Feedback and support requests

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, operating system, app version)
- Usage statistics (features used, processing times)
- Crash reports and performance data
- IP address and network information

### Information Not Collected
- We DO NOT collect:
  - Content of your PDF files or documents
  - Personal identification information
  - Location data
  - Contacts or other personal data

## How We Use Your Information

### Processing Operations
- To perform PDF conversion, editing, and optimization
- To provide the requested file processing services
- To improve app performance and user experience

### Analytics and Improvements
- To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
- To identify and fix bugs and performance issues
- To develop new features based on user needs

### Communication
- To respond to your support requests
- To notify you about important updates

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Processing
- All file processing occurs locally on your device
- Files are NOT uploaded to any servers
- No third parties have access to your documents

### Data Retention
- Processed files are automatically deleted after operations
- Temporary files are removed when the app is closed
- No permanent storage of your documents on our systems

## Third-Party Services

### Analytics
We may use anonymous analytics services to improve our app:
- Usage statistics collection
- Performance monitoring
- Crash reporting

### Cloud Services
Some features may use cloud services for:
- Temporary file processing
- Synchronization across devices (if enabled)
- Backup and restore functionality (if enabled)

## Your Rights

### Data Access and Control
- You can delete processed files at any time
- You can clear app data and cache through device settings
- You can uninstall the app to remove all local data

### Opt-Out Options
- Disable analytics collection in app settings
- Turn off notifications in device settings
- Uninstall the app to stop all data collection

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

This Privacy Policy complies with:
- Google Play Store Developer Policy
